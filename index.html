What are stochastic differential equations (SDEs)?

SDEs extend the theory of (ordinary) differential equations (DE) to include a source of randomness1

  • DEs model a system’s dynamics and depend on system’s states, inputs, parameters, and time

Aims of the research

With the theory of SDEs we aim to:

  • model systems influenced by random fluctuations;
  • simulate computationally and analyse systems described by SDEs;
  • estimate processes, leveraging noise as a source of uncertainty;
  • control systems affected by random inputs.

Some objectives were partially achieved:

  • the stochastic modelling and simulation of chemical reactions12;
  • the estimation of the parameters of a model of bioreactor3
